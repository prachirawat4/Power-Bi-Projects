This repository showcases two end-to-end Power BI dashboards focused on business performance analytics — one for Blinkit Sales Performance and another for Inventory & Supply Chain Management.
Both dashboards demonstrate strong analytical thinking, DAX proficiency, and storytelling skills for real-world decision-making.

 Project 1: Blinklytics:Blinkit Sales Performance Dashboard
 Overview

This dashboard provides a comprehensive sales analysis for Blinkit, India’s last-minute delivery app.
It offers insights into total sales, outlet performance, item categories, and customer behavior, helping the business identify growth opportunities and improve retail efficiency.

 Key Metrics & Insights

 Total Sales: $1.20M

 Total Items Sold: 8,523

 Average Sales: $141 | Average Rating: 3.9

 Top Performing Categories: Fruits & Vegetables, Snack Foods ($0.18M each)

 Highest Outlet Sales: Tier 3 locations ($472.13K) | Medium-sized outlets ($507.90K)

 Visuals & Analysis

Sales by Fat Content: Pie chart comparing Low Fat ($425.36K) vs Regular Fat ($776.32K)

Item Type Sales Breakdown: Bar chart of category-wise sales

Outlet Establishment Trend: Line chart showing sales growth over time

Outlet Type Comparison: Analysis of Grocery vs Supermarket performance

Interactive Filters: Location Type, Outlet Size, Item Type

 Skills Demonstrated

Power Query data transformation

DAX for KPIs (Total Sales, Avg Rating, Sales per Outlet)

Hierarchical filtering and drill-downs

Professional UI design & color consistency

 Project 2: SupplySphere: Inventory & Supply Chain Analysis Dashboard
 Overview

Managing inventory and supply chain operations is crucial for profitability and customer satisfaction.
This project focuses on stock management, demand forecasting, supplier performance, and logistics optimization, built as a fully interactive Power BI dashboard.

 Project Highlights

 Data Cleaning & Modeling: Power Query transformations and star schema design

 Inventory Metrics: Stock levels, turnover rates, reorder points, and backorders

 Supply Chain KPIs: Lead time, supplier reliability, delivery accuracy

 Advanced DAX: Predictive and time intelligence calculations

 Dashboard Design: Executive-level visuals and drill-through capabilities

 Storytelling: Real-world insights for procurement and warehouse management

 Skills Demonstrated

Power BI data modeling & relationship management

Advanced DAX (CALCULATE, FILTER, RELATED, RANKX)

Supply chain data interpretation

Visual storytelling for business users

 Tools & Technologies

Power BI Desktop

Microsoft Excel / CSV Datasets

Power Query (ETL)

DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)

Data Visualization & Storytelling
